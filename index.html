About Me

I am a PhD Computer Science student at North Carolina State University advised by Dr. Anupam Das. I am a member of Wolfpack Security and Privacy Lab (WSPR) at NC State Computer Science department. Previously, I graduated with BS(CS) from Lahore University of Management Sciences in 2020. My research interests lie in Security and Privacy domain. I am currently working on analyzing and mitigating privacy and security issues in smart home devices and voice assistants which involves understanding the gap between user mental model and system behavior.
